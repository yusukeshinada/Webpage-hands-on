Laser Print Introduction

Interaction design, Education

Tools Used : Illustrator, Laser printers

The laser printer makes me be able to carve patterns whatever you want on objects or cut off complicated shapes without cutting the edge of it. and this technology have been today used with a wide range regardless of the industries.


At ITP in New York University

The process:


First of all I had a sheet and designed something I like to print by illustrator. Sending its data to the laser printer is as easy as a typical printer expected to be in the office.

The next stop is to set up the objects where you wanna print and a little bit difficult bcz we're supposed to measure a distance btw the output of laser and the object using the special ruler.



The rectangle in red is the range in which the design is printed.


The movie's playing at the speeds of 10 times faster. Actual time till the end took about 5 minites.
